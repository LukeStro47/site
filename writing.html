Dissertation

The Psychedelic Experience as a Sublime Aesthetic

Queen's University Belfast · 2025 · Monsignor Arthur H. Ryan Award

My undergraduate dissertation draws on Kant's and Burke's accounts of the sublime to analyse the structure of the psychedelic experience — its vastness, its dissolution of boundaries, and its mixture of terror and transcendence. It was awarded the Monsignor Arthur H. Ryan Prize for the highest final mark in undergraduate philosophy and presented at PsychedelX 2025, the largest student psychedelics conference.
